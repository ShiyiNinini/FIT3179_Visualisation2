Where People Live vs Where Beds Are

This dashboard demonstrates the relationship between population distribution and healthcare infrastructure across Malaysia

Healthcare Infrastructure Overview

Hospital Beds Distribution

This choropleth map shows the distribution of hospital beds per 1,000 people across Malaysian states. The color intensity represents the bed-to-population ratio, with darker colors indicating higher bed availability.

Use the year slider to explore how this distribution has changed over time from 2015 to 2022. This visualization helps identify regions with adequate healthcare infrastructure versus those that may need more investment.

Population Demographics Analysis

Demographic Insights

The left chart shows population distribution by age groups across states, revealing which regions have younger or older populations. This is crucial for healthcare planning as different age groups have varying healthcare needs.

The right chart displays population by ethnicity and state, showing the diverse demographic composition of Malaysia. Understanding these patterns helps in planning culturally appropriate healthcare services.

Regional Trends and Comparisons

Regional Analysis

This section compares trends between West and East Malaysia, showing how population and healthcare infrastructure have evolved differently across regions.

The total trends chart reveals overall patterns in population growth and hospital bed availability, while the regional comparison highlights disparities between the two main regions of Malaysia.
